Murphy is the Best Cat

He Is My Best Friend

I adopted my cat Murphy when he was 5 years old. He still acts like a kitten, but also knows how to nap like nobody's business. He follows my all over the house, and loves to yell at me. He loves to snuggle, especially in the mornings. Belly rubs are his favorite, they make him drool all over the place.

Nobody's Perfect...

  • He is always starving
  • We had to child lock the cabinets because of him
  • Sometimes he will attack you if he is grumpy
  • He has stinky breath
Everyone needs a Murphy.
